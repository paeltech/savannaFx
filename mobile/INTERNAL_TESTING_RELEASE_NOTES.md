## üêõ Known Issues & Limitations

### Current Limitations
1. **Payment Integration**
   - In-app purchases require Google Play Billing integration (pending)
   - Subscription management needs Play Store integration

2. **WhatsApp Notifications**
   - Phone verification system in development
   - WhatsApp integration requires phone number verification

3. **Telegram Integration**
   - Telegram notifications coming soon
   - Currently disabled in preferences

4. **iOS Support**
   - iOS version coming in next release
   - Currently Android-only

### Known Issues
- Some screens may show loading states during initial data fetch
- Notification preferences may require app restart to fully apply
- Large signal lists may take a moment to load on slower connections
